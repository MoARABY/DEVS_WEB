who we are?

A programming community affiliated with Suez canal University and headquartered in the Faculty of Computers and Information . It was founded in 2023 and was officially approved by the administration of the Faculty of Computers and Information in the tenth month of the same year,The main goal of establishing the group is to make the most of the university stage and prepare a member ready for the labor market with strong CV and recommended soft skills.

what we do!

After the student passes the requirements and tests to join the group, he is added to a team of the teams within the group , where the team consists of several members, each of whom works integrated and helpful with the others, as projects are distributed to each team within the group and the instructions, standards, and timings necessary for delivery are given. Then the projects are evaluated , and gradually the highest-rated teams are selected and qualified for the “simulated work.”
